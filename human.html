Hello

I’m a UX/UI Designer based (soon!) in Paris. With empathy and compassion I help design products and experiences that empower people via functional simplicity and moments of joy. I strive to be a strong advocate for ethical design and push to balance the needs of business with moral and socially conscious decisions.

I love taking photos, reading, and drinking oatmilk cortados. I also really love my cat, Kitten.

Curriculum Vitae

Background

I studied Media Arts at the University of New Mexico as well as Filmmaking at the Santa Fe University of Art and Design.

I’ve worked for five years as a Set Costumer in both LA and Albuquerque, NM as a proud IATSE union member. Before that, I worked as a UI Designer at Apex Evaluation.

After re-examining my passions and priorities amidst COVID lockdowns, I’ve decided to delve back into the world of design. I completed an intensive 10-week UX/UI bootcamp with Ironhack Paris. I’m so excited to again be working in a creative field with the ability to truly touch people’s lives through the products they use and experiences they live.
